PERSPECTIVES

Some of my favorite life lessons, perspectives, and frameworks of thinking.

Life is too short to be small.

If you're going to dream, you might as well dream big.

Life is already short. You can choose whether you want it small or big. It’s a miracle you’re alive. We have one life and only one shot to live.

No matter who you are, you can have an impact on at least one person. You’re going to live the time you're going to live, so why choose to have a small impact if you can have a big impact? After you cease to exist you don't take anything with you, but what you leave stays.

Step up the consciousness staircase.

What I think is the umbrella goal for humanity.

A dog is more conscious than a cow. And a cow is more conscious than a beetle. A human is more conscious than a dog, but can there be something more conscious than said human? Some aliens or other humans. Realizing that there’s different consciousness steps even within our human level is, I think, the best way to approach personal growth. And I think that it should also be our goal collectively as humans.

The goal of personal growth should be to gain that deathbed clarity while your life is still happening so you can actually do something about it. The way you do that is by developing as much wisdom as possible, as early as possible. To me, wisdom is the most important thing to work towards as a human. It’s the big objective—the umbrella goal under which all other goals fall into place.

Understand other people

Just because people make different choices than you, they are not stupid. Also, you don’t know everything about people, so don’t judge them — help them.

Wisdom sheds light on what fulfillment and meaningful mean

After reading several books and talking with a lot of people, even some graduating in “how to live a meaningful and fulfilled life” or “happiness”, I think what people are more universally looking for, and usually confused with happiness, is fulfillment.

Wisdom gives you the insight to know what “fulfillment” and “meaningful” actually mean and the courage to make the choices that will take you there.
